Lasagna

is a recipe for lasagna, very easy concept that I do not need to explain to you.

Lasagna is an Italian dish which is made up of layers of cheese, sauce, and beef.

Here are the ingredients you will need:

  • Sweet Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • Large white onion diced
  • 5 cloves of garlic minced
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • Half a cup of white wine
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Hlaf a cup of chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 30 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 pound thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Put all of the ingredients on a crafting table
  2. Click on the craft button
  3. Share the lasagna with your friends
  4. Eat!
