French Toast

French toast is bread coated in egg, served with butter, and is often sweet

There are many ways to make french toast, but here is a simple recipe to help you make your first french toast.

Here are the ingredients you will need before you start

  • White bread
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. crack your eggs into a large bowl
  2. put some salt and sugar into the eggs
  3. dip your bread into the mixture
  4. put the dipped bread into a pan with butter in it
  5. cook until ready
  6. serve with knob of butter on the toast
  7. gulf it down like an animal
