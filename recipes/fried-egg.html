Fried Egg

A fried egg is one of the simplest dishes to make, so I'm not sure why you're here. But since you are already here, here's some instructions on how to make one.

Ingredients you will need:

  • eggs
  • oil of any kind

Steps:

  1. put some oil in a pan on medium heat and let it heat up
  2. crack open your egg into the pan
  3. wait until cooked, flip if you want to
  4. eat it like the caveman you are
